home about me research



hi there! i am a senior at florida state university majoring in psychology and biomathematics. my research interest centers on understanding the dynamic interplay between our past experiences, current perceptions, and future thinking. i am particularly interested in exploring the mechanisms through which our current experiences become integrated with our past and how our past experiences influence the encoding of new memories and shape the way we perceive and think about the future. i hope to pursue a Ph.D. in cognitive neuroscience to study these topics further by integrating computational modeling and empirical methods (fMRI, EEG, behavioral studies).


currently, i am an undergraduate research assistant in the martin memory lab where i am studying how we selectively retrieve semantic and episodic information using fMRI.
