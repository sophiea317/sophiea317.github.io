hi there! i am a senior at florida state university majoring in psychology and biomathematics. my interests encompass human long-term memory, perception, and learning. specifically, i am fascinated by the neural principles that guide these topics, how these principles change throughout our lifespan, and how factors at encoding and reactivation during sleep and wake play a pivotal role in shaping long-term memory. i hope to pursue a Ph.D. in cognitive neuroscience to study these topics further by integrating computational modeling and empirical methods (fMRI, EEG, behavioral studies).


currently, i am an undergraduate research assistant in the martin memory lab where I am studying how we selectively retrieve semantic and episodic information using fMRI.
